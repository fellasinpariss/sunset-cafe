Menu


Coffees and Donuts


Experience the enchantment of our Sunset Café menu, where every sip tells a story and every bite of our artisanal donuts takes you on a flavorful journey.


Vanilla Latte

A smooth and comforting blend of espresso and steamed milk infused with the sweet essence of vanilla.

Iced Americano

A refreshing and bold iced coffee made by diluting rich espresso with cold water, perfect for warm days.

Caramel Macchiato

An indulgent treat featuring espresso poured over creamy milk and vanilla syrup, topped with a drizzle of caramel.

Matcha Espresso Fusion

A unique combination of vibrant matcha and robust espresso, creating a harmonious blend of earthy and bold flavors.

Caffè Americano

A classic coffee experience made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, resulting in a strong and satisfying cup.

Macchiato

An espresso "stained" with a dollop of frothy milk, delivering a strong coffee flavor with a touch of creaminess.

Caffè Mocha

A decadent treat featuring espresso, steamed milk, and rich chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream for added indulgence.

Pumpkin Spice Latte

Fall in a cup, this latte combines espresso and steamed milk with pumpkin spice syrup, delivering a warm and spiced flavor.

Egg Coffee

A unique concoction where coffee is combined with a whipped mixture of egg and condensed milk, resulting in a creamy and velvety beverage.

Eiskaffee

A German delight featuring chilled coffee served over vanilla ice cream, creating a refreshing and indulgent summer drink.

Flat Coffee

A simple and straightforward black coffee, emphasizing the pure, unadulterated flavor of freshly brewed beans.

Café Affogato

A delightful dessert drink where a shot of hot espresso is poured over a scoop of vanilla ice cream, creating a delightful contrast of temperatures and textures.

Classic Glazed

A simple yet irresistible donut coated in a sweet and shiny vanilla glaze.

Chocolate Frosted

A chocolate lover's delight, featuring a chocolate glaze that adds a rich and decadent touch.

Strawberry Sprinkle

A strawberry-flavored donut with a vibrant pink glaze and colorful sprinkles for a fun twist.

Vanilla cream Twist

A twist-shaped donut with a rich vanilla cream flavor, perfect for those who love a classic taste.

Cinnamon Sugar Delight

Coated in a generous layer of cinnamon sugar, this donut offers a warm and comforting flavor.

Double Chocolate Chip

For the ultimate chocolate experience, this donut is studded with chocolate chips and coated in a chocolate glaze.

Blueberry Blast

The Blueberry Blast Donut is a delightful treat with a soft blueberry-infused center and a sweet, vibrant blueberry glaze.

Maple Glazed Old Fashioned

A nod to tradition, this donut features a maple glaze on an old-fashioned-style cake donut.

Caramel Crunch Bliss

A caramel-flavored donut topped with a crunchy layer of caramelized sugar for a delightful texture.

Lemon Zest Temptation

Zesty and refreshing, this donut is infused with lemon flavor and topped with a tangy lemon glaze and sweet lavender.

Coffee Creme Filled

A coffee-infused donut filled with a smooth and creamy coffee-flavored filling.

Raspberry Jelly Joy

A classic ring donut filled with sweet and tart raspberry jelly, creating a burst of fruity flavor with every bite.
